About this home

Built in 2019 on a tree-lined Willow Glen street, 1232 Delmas Ave this home blends neighborhood charm with like-new living and major upgrades, just half a mile to Downtown Lincoln Ave for dining and shops. A fenced front yard and porch open to a two-story entry and grand staircase. The main level offers a true bedroom and full bath, & light-filled great room with two French doors to the backyard.

The kitchen is designed for everyday living & entertaining, feat. an oversized island and Thermador suite with double ovens & 6-burner range.

Upstairs are 2 bedrooms with walk-in closets, laundry on the same level, and a luxe primary with dual vanities, soaking tub, walk-in shower, & two walk-in closets. Downstairs, the basement features 9-ft ceilings, glass- door wine storage, and projector wiring - ideal for movie nights, a gym, or a game room.

Out back, enjoy a pergola, turf, privacy hedges, a gas line for a fire table, and a 2023 luxury outdoor kitchen with quartzite counters, BBQ, gas burner, and fridge. Remote driveway gate + covered side drive add extra parking and lead to a finished 2-car garage with epoxy floors. Owned Tesla solar w/2 backup batteries, EV wiring, Hunter Douglas blinds, fire sprinklers, & water softener. Easy access to HWY 280 & 87, DT San Jose, Tamien Caltrain Station, & more!
